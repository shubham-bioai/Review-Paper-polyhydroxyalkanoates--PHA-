## Review Scope Definition

### Included Topics
- Microbial polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHAs)
- Cost-related challenges in PHA production
- Scalability and commercialization barriers
- Genetic and metabolic engineering approaches
- Synthetic biology strategies
- Use of low-cost and waste-based substrates
- Sustainability and life cycle assessment perspectives

### Excluded Topics
- Plant-based bioplastics (PLA, starch, etc.)
- Purely chemical polymer synthesis
- Market pricing and business model analysis
- Experimental or lab-generated data

### Review Focus
This review critically analyzes why microbial PHAs have faced challenges in commercial-scale adoption despite promising laboratory results, and how biotechnological innovations can address these limitations.

### Intended Audience
- Biotechnology students and researchers
- Industrial biotechnology professionals
- Sustainability and bioplastics researchers
